Gilbert Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Gilbert Chamber of Commerce is an organization devoted to building a community that supports locally owned businesses. We offer networking opportunities and access to community events to help build relationships and strengthen the local economy.

The main colors for the site will be white and blue, with the bulk of the text being in a soft dark brown. White will be used for text on blue, brown or green backgrounds. The navigation menu will be green, the log in button will be orange and the object boxes will be headlined with blue. The footer will be blue.

Typography

DM Serif Display will be used for headers and buttons. Roboto font will be used for the main text for the page as well as the menu and navigation links.
