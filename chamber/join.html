Membership Details

Event Discounts

We can offer discounts to many community events that will allow your small business to get out there and be seen by local community members.

Spotlight Advertising

Our main website has space for business spotlights to help you advertise your products and services to other Chamber members. This gives you a wide exposure to like minded people to facilitate networking amoung small busniesses.

Personalized Training

We offer personalized training to help you and your employees improve their specialzed and business related skills. We contract with a variety of Specialists and Professional Education Organizations to provide you a wide array of resources to help step up your business game.

Special Events Access

Our Chamber sets up multiple Special Events each year for our Gold Members to facilitate networking and build community between small business owners. You will have access to these exclusive events at the Gold Membership Level.
