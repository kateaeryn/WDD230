Days Since Last Visit

Gilbert's Historical Liberty Market

Established as a grocery store in the early 1930s, became an urban bistro in 1981 known for their diversified and refined menu

Local Gilbert Events

From Gilbert Days and fun runs to movies in the park, there is always something fun happening in Gilbert

Gilbert's Diverse Natural World

Gilbert's Riparian Preserve works to educate about the natural wildlife, flora and fauna of the desert, which surprisingly includes water animals

Gilbert Water Recreation and Pools

Being in a desert, it does get hot and there are tons of places to cool down, from local pools and aquatic centers to water parks

Gilbert Museums

Gilbert's Historical Museum walks you through the history of our town and the surrounding area

Hale Theater

The Famous Hale Theater, orginally opened in 1947, is one of five theaters run by members of the Hale family. It is a traditional theater-in-the-round once popular in Greece and Rome
